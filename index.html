How to dress for your dream job- November 16th

We had the wonderful pleasure of interviwing a megastar on how to dress for an interview

Last week, I met with QueenB to discuss her picks for the chilly days ahead.

ME: So B, what advice do you have for your fashionistas out there?

QB: Knits. Knits with denim; knits with sweats. Knits is what I live by, at this time of year.

ME: If you could shop at just one shop for the rest of your life, which one would it be?

QB: That’s easy: H&M. Affordable, comfortable and fashionable. If in doubt, H&M.

ME: And one last one; any advice for a beginner?

QB: Follow my insta and read this blog. You can’t go wrong.
