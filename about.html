About Us

Petite a la mode is your go-to place for all things petite.

We are a new and rising fashion blog specialising in fashion advice for women who are 5’3 and under. Never let your size get it the way of unleashing your personal style. Whether you are looking for the latest fashion trends, want to find that gorgeous date night dress, or are looking for travel inspiration for your next getaway, we’ve got it all covered. Our latest style tips will save you from ever worrying about what you should wear. We will guide you throughout, even as the seasons change.
